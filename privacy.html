Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2024

Plain English summary: Drive File Uploader uploads files directly to your own Google Drive. We do not collect, store, or share your files, file names, or personal data. Your data stays in your Google account.

1. Who we are

Drive File Uploader ("the Add-on", "we", "our") is a Google Sheets add-on that allows users to upload files from their local device to their own Google Drive account and insert shareable links into their Google Sheets.

For privacy questions, contact us at: support@example.com

2. What data we access

To function, the Add-on requests the following Google OAuth permissions:

  • Google Drive — to create files in your Drive and set sharing permissions on those files only.
  • Google Sheets — to read the active cell address and write the Drive link into that cell.
  • Script UI — to display the sidebar panel inside Google Sheets.

We access only the minimum permissions required for the add-on to function.

3. What data we do NOT collect

  • We do not store any files you upload.
  • We do not read the content of your uploaded files.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or Google account information.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking tools.
  • We do not sell or share your data with any third party.

4. Upload history storage

The Add-on stores a lightweight upload history (file name, timestamp, and Drive link) using Google Apps Script's DocumentProperties service. This data is stored exclusively within your Google document and is never transmitted to any external server. You can delete this history at any time using the "Clear all" button in the sidebar.

5. How your files are handled

When you upload a file:

  • The file is read locally in your browser and transmitted directly to Google's servers via the Apps Script runtime.
  • The file is saved into the Google Drive folder you select — which belongs to your Google account.
  • The Add-on sets the file's sharing to "Anyone with the link can view" so the link works when inserted into your sheet. You can change these permissions in Google Drive at any time.
  • We never receive, process, or store a copy of your file on any third-party server.

6. Google API Services

Drive File Uploader's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

7. Children's privacy

This Add-on is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the Add-on after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
