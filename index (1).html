How it works
Three steps from file to link.
1
Open the sidebar

In Google Sheets, go to Extensions → Drive File Uploader → Open File Uploader. The sidebar appears on the right side of your sheet.

2
Select your file & folder

Click the active cell where you want the link, then drag your file into the drop zone or click to browse. Choose a destination folder from the dropdown.

3
Click Upload — done

The file uploads to Google Drive, becomes shareable, and the link lands directly in your cell. The history panel logs the upload with a clickable link.
